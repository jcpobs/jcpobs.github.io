How We Start

We started being consumers of Relx way back in 2020. We frequently buy from an online merchant near us, but the supply of devices is limited and the available flavors are only selected. Imagine the hassle of going to the seller every time we need juice. It's so consuming that's why we start being resellers yet unauthorized.

At first, we were just a small reseller. We get our product from an online merchant and resell it. In a short amount of time, the sales drastically increased, and that was the moment we decided to become an authorized reseller of Relx.

We are the first shop in our area that sells multiple flavors and different varieties of devices. Having this opportunity, we can guarantee our customers that our product is safe and authentic. That is the best selling point of our store.
